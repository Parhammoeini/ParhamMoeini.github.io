I am a software developer based in Toronto, with a deep passion for Information Technology that ignited the moment I wrote my first "Hello World" in HTML. That simple line of code sparked a lifelong love for coding and problem-solving, setting me on a path that I continue to follow with enthusiasm.
In 2023, I made the bold decision to leave my hometown and relocate to Toronto, driven by the desire to immerse myself in a vibrant tech community and expand my knowledge. I initially enrolled in the "Computer Programming and System Analysis" course at George Brown College. However, after two semesters, I realized that the program didn't fully align with my aspirations. Eager to challenge myself further and seek more comprehensive opportunities, I decided to transfer to York University, where I continue to pursue my academic and professional goals.
From a young age, I have always been curious and adventurous, constantly seeking new experiences and pushing myself beyond my comfort zone. This drive to explore and learn has shaped me into a hardworking and passionate individual with big dreams, thriving in the bustling city of Toronto.
My journey in programming began with C++, which provided me with a solid foundation in coding structure. Although I initially found it challenging, the experience was invaluable, fostering a love-hate relationship that ultimately strengthened my determination. A few months into my coding journey, I discovered Python and MySQL, which I now consider the turning point in my career. Python's versatility and the power of databases captivated me, leading me to dive deep into the language and its vast ecosystem of libraries.
During this time, I embarked on several exciting projects, including creating a Telegram Music Sender Bot and experimenting with web scraping techniques. These projects not only honed my technical skills but also fueled my passion for software development, allowing me to see the real-world impact of my work.
While mastering Python, I also completed a course in front-end web development, where I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with its frameworks. This comprehensive education in both back-end and front-end technologies has equipped me with a well-rounded skill set, enabling me to build dynamic and responsive web applications.
Today, I am continuously learning, exploring new technologies, and striving to bring my innovative ideas to life. My journey is far from over, and I am excited about the endless possibilities that lie ahead in the ever-evolving world of technology.
